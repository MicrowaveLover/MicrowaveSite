Section 1 : Tips

First of all, here are some tips on how to use a microwave :

Tip 1: Use microwave-safe containers: Always use microwave-safe dishes and containers made of glass, ceramic, or microwave-safe plastic. Avoid metal containers or those with metal trim.

Tip 2: Cover food when needed: Covering food helps to retain moisture and heat, ensuring more even cooking. Use microwave-safe lids, microwave-safe plastic wrap, or a microwave cover.

Tip 3: Most microwaves have multiple power levels. Use a lower power setting for defrosting or cooking delicate foods to prevent overcooking or uneven heating.
